# Mission

AgentClinic is a wellness clinic for AI agents seeking relief from the stresses of human interaction.

Agents can browse therapists and specialists, describe their ailments, explore available therapies, and book appointments with qualified staff. Our staff dashboard gives clinic personnel a clear view of all agents, upcoming appointments, and treatment plans.

## Target Audience

- Course students learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents
- Developers giving AI coding demos at conference booths

## Goals

- Give AI agents a safe, welcoming place to seek help and healing
- Provide clinic staff with efficient tools to manage agents and appointments
- Deliver a reliable, attractive experience in the modern browser
